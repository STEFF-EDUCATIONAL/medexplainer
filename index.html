Informational use only. MedExplainer provides simplified explanations of user-provided medical information (e.g., lab values, symptoms, documents). It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment and does not replace consultation with a qualified physician.
Emergency notice: If you think you may have a medical emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.
What MedExplainer does Clear explanations
  • Explains medical terms in plain language and highlights what each part of a report typically means.
  • Structures your documents (labs, imaging reports, discharge summaries) into an easy-to-read overview.
  • Provides context using age information to help interpret common reference ranges and patterns (informational, not diagnostic).
  • Creates a shareable report and allows PDF export for discussion with a healthcare professional.
What MedExplainer does NOT do Safety first
  • No diagnosis. The Service does not diagnose conditions or determine what disease you have.
  • No treatment plans. The Service does not prescribe, recommend, or adjust medications or therapies.
  • No emergency triage. The Service is not intended for emergencies or urgent medical decision-making.
  • No server-side history. The Service does not maintain a medical history or longitudinal record on the server in Phase 1–2.
Data handling (Phase 1–2) Minimal data
  • No registration required. You can use the Service without creating an account.
  • Stateless AI requests. Your inputs are processed to generate the response and are not saved as a server-side history.
  • Local storage only. If you choose to save a report, it is stored on your device (not on our server).
  • No ads / no trackers. No advertising identifiers and no analytics SDKs for tracking users across apps.
For details, see Privacy Policy.
Designed for everyday family care B2C focus
  • For women 30+ who often coordinate health decisions for children and parents.
  • Clarity reduces anxiety. When you understand reports better, conversations with doctors become more productive.
  • Better questions for appointments. Export a clean summary and discuss it with a licensed professional.
