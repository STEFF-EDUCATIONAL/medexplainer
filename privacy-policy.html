1) Overview

MedExplainer is an informational tool that generates AI-based explanations of medical information provided voluntarily by users.

2) Information You Provide

  • Patient age (required for contextual explanation).
  • Optional name (not required).
  • Symptoms or medical questions entered in text form.
  • Uploaded files such as PDF/DOC and images.

3) How Processing Works

Submitted data is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to the backend for processing. The backend forwards requests to a third-party AI provider (OpenAI API) to generate explanations.

We do not permanently store:

  • Medical text
  • Uploaded documents
  • Generated explanations

Temporary processing files (e.g., for OCR) may be created and are deleted immediately after processing. The system is session-based and stateless.

4) Local Storage

Saved reports are stored only on your device. No cloud medical record storage is provided.

5) Tracking & Advertising

We do not use tracking technologies, advertising identifiers, or cross-app tracking.

6) Age Restriction

The Service is intended for users aged 16+.
