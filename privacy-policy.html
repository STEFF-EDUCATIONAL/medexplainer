MedExplainer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026
Data Controller / Operator: MEDEXPLAINER (Republic of Cyprus)
Contact: support@medexplainer.online

1. Overview

MedExplainer is an educational application designed to provide simplified explanations of medical information entered by users. The application is intended strictly for informational and educational purposes only. MedExplainer does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or clinical decision support.

2. Information You Provide

You may voluntarily provide the following information:

  • Patient age (required to display age-appropriate reference ranges).
  • Optional name or identifier (not required).
  • Symptom descriptions or medical questions entered in text form.
  • Uploaded documents such as PDF, DOC, or medical images (e.g., lab reports, X-ray, ultrasound).

3. How Data Is Processed

When a user submits a request, the data is securely transmitted via HTTPS to MedExplainer’s backend server. The backend forwards the request to a third-party AI processing provider (OpenAI API) for the sole purpose of generating an explanatory response. The generated explanation is then returned to the user’s device.

MedExplainer does not permanently store:

  • Symptom descriptions
  • Uploaded documents
  • Medical images
  • Generated explanations

Uploaded files may be temporarily processed in memory for analysis purposes and are automatically deleted immediately after processing. No medical data is retained on MedExplainer servers.

4. Data Storage

MedExplainer does not require user registration in the current version. All data processing is session-based. Users may optionally save generated explanations locally on their device. MedExplainer does not maintain cloud storage of medical records.

5. Third-Party Processing

MedExplainer uses OpenAI API as a data processor solely to generate AI-based explanations. OpenAI processes requests in accordance with its own privacy and security policies. MedExplainer does not use medical data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or analytics.

6. Tracking and Advertising

MedExplainer does not use tracking technologies, advertising identifiers (IDFA/IDFV), cross-app tracking, or third-party advertising SDKs. No health data is shared for advertising purposes.

7. Data Security

All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS. Temporary processing files are automatically deleted after use. MedExplainer applies reasonable technical safeguards to protect data during transmission.

8. Medical Disclaimer

MedExplainer provides informational explanations only and does not replace consultation with a licensed healthcare professional.

Responses are generated automatically by an artificial intelligence system and may contain inaccuracies or incomplete information.

If you experience severe or urgent symptoms, seek immediate medical attention.

9. Children’s Privacy

MedExplainer is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Future Updates

If MedExplainer introduces user accounts, cloud storage, or expanded data functionality in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any material changes will be reflected prior to release of such features.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@medexplainer.online.
