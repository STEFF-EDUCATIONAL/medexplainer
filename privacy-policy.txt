Privacy Policy for MEDEXPLAINER
Last updated: 2026-02-08
MEDEXPLAINER respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected,
how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the MEDEXPLAINER mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
MEDEXPLAINER may process the following information only when you voluntarily provide it:
• Patient age
• Patient name (optional)
• Medical questions entered as text
• Medical documents uploaded by the user (PDF files and images such as photos, X-ray,
ultrasound)
The application does not require account registration.
MEDEXPLAINER does not require users to provide personally identifiable information such as full
name, address, phone number, or contact details.

2. How Information Is Used
The information you provide is used solely to generate an informational medical explanation within
the application.
Specifically:
• Data may be temporarily transmitted to automated AI processing services to generate an
informational response.
• The generated content may be displayed on screen and optionally exported by the user as a PDF
file.
MEDEXPLAINER does not store user data permanently, create user profiles, maintain medical
histories, or use provided data for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Medical Disclaimer
MEDEXPLAINER provides informational content generated by automated systems only.
It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or clinical decision support.
The application is not a medical device.
The information provided is intended for educational purposes only and must not be used as a
substitute for professional medical consultation.

4. User Responsibility
The user is solely responsible for how they interpret and use the information provided by the
application.

5. Data Storage and Retention
User-provided data is processed only for the duration of the request.
MEDEXPLAINER does not store uploaded documents, medical data, or analysis results on its own
servers.
Exported PDF files are stored only on the user’s device if the user chooses to save them.

6. Third-Party Services
MEDEXPLAINER uses trusted third-party services to operate the application, including:
• AI processing services to generate informational responses
• Google AdMob for displaying advertisements
These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Advertising
The application displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.
MEDEXPLAINER does not share medical content or uploaded documents with advertisers.

8. Children’s Privacy
MEDEXPLAINER is not intended for use by children without the involvement and supervision of a
parent or legal guardian.
MEDEXPLAINER does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security
Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information during
processing.
However, no method of data transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected with an updated revision date.
